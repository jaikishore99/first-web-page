SEVEN WONDERS

The seven wonders of the world are impressive monuments regarded with awe and wonder. ... The new seven modern wonders of the world are comprised of: the Taj Mahal of India, The Great Wall of China, Christ the Redeemer statue of Brazil, Machu Picchu of Peru, Petra of Jordan, Colosseum of Italy, and Chichen Itza of Mexico.

1 . MACHU PICCHU

Machu Picchu is a pre-Columbian 15th-century Inca site in Peru,in South America.

The Incas built the city on a mountain ridge, 2430m above sea level.

Machu Picchu is very difficult to get to because it is so high in the mountains.

It has only one way in and a stone wall to protect it.

Location: Peru; Cusco Region

2 . GREAT WALL OF CHINA

It was built to protect the north of the empire of China from enemy attacks.

It is the longest structure humans have ever built.

It is about 21,196 kilometres (13,171 miles) long, 9.1 metres (30 feet) wide and 15 metres (50 feet) high.

The earlier sections on the wall are made of compacted dirt and stone.

Location:Huairou District, China

3 . TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is a white tomb built in the 16th century by the Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

The building is in the city of Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

It is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, together with the Agra Fort, 2.5 kilometers away.

Taj Mahal Location. It's the city of Agra that has received the honor of having one of Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal: an abode for eternal Love, in its courtyard.

Located in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India, Agra has been one powerful city since the medieval times.

4 . COLOSSEUM

The Colosseum, originally known as the Flavian Amphitheatre.

it is a large amphitheatre in the city of Rome.

The construction of the Colosseum started around 70–72 AD and was finished in 80 AD.

Emperor Vespasian started the work, and Emperor Titus completed it.

... It is the biggest amphitheatre built by the Roman Empire.

Location:Rome

5 . PETRA

The ancient city of Petra is a famous archaeological site in Jordan's southwestern desert.

Petra is famous for its tombs, temples, churches and houses which are all carved into pink sandstone cliffs;

earning its nickname, the “Rose City”.

Location:Located amid rugged desert canyons and mountains in what is now the southwestern corner of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan

6 . CHRIST THE REDEEMER

Christ the Redeemer is the largest art deco statue in the world.

It is 98 feet tall (not including the 26 foot pedestal), and the arms stretch to 92 feet wide.

Christ the Redeemer is located in the Tijuca Forest National Forest, at the top of the Corcovado Mountain.

Location:brazil

7 . CHICHEN ITZA

Chichén Itzá is a large Mayan city famous for a large, pyramid temple built by the Maya civilization.

It is on the Yucatán Peninsula, about 120 km to the east of Mérida.

The temple, called Castillo, is about 1 km in diameter.

There are many temples and pyramids at Chichen Itza.

Location:Mexico
